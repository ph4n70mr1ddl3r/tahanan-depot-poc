## 26.1 CSR Governance and Strategy

### 26.1.1 CSR Vision and Mission

| Element | Statement |
|---|---|
| **CSR Vision** | A nation where every Filipino family lives in a safe, sustainable, and dignified home — built with materials sourced responsibly and delivered by a company that cares for people and planet. |
| **CSR Mission** | To leverage Tahanan Depot's retail reach, supply chain infrastructure, and 6,500-strong workforce to advance environmental stewardship, community resilience, inclusive growth, and sustainable home improvement across the Philippines. |
| **CSR Tagline** | "Tahanan: Tahanan ng Pamilyang Pilipino, Tahanan ng Kalikasan" (Home of the Filipino Family, Home of Nature) |

### 26.1.2 CSR Governance Structure

| Level | Body | Composition | Meeting Frequency | Authority |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Board Level | CSR Committee of the Board | 3 independent directors + CEO + CSO | Quarterly | Approve CSR strategy, budget, targets, annual report |

## 26.6 Sustainable Sourcing

### 26.6.1 Supplier Code of Conduct

> **Cross-reference:** POL-CSR-007 (Supplier Code of Conduct); Doc 12 (Master Data Governance) — Supplier Sustainability Data

**Code of Conduct Core Principles:**

| Principle | Requirement | Verification Method | Non-Compliance Consequence |
|---|---|---|---|
| Legal Compliance | Comply with all Philippine laws and regulations in jurisdictions of operation | Annual self-certification + spot audit | Corrective action plan (CAP) within 30 days; suspension if unresolved |
| Environmental Responsibility | Implement environmental management practices; reduce waste, emissions, water usage | ISO 14001 certification or equivalent; audit | CAP; supplier development program |
| Labor Rights | No forced labor, child labor, or human trafficking; comply with Philippine Labor Code | Document review + worker interviews during audit | Immediate suspension |
| Fair Wages | Pay at least minimum wage; provide legally mandated benefits | Payroll records review | CAP within 15 days |
| Occupational Health & Safety | Maintain safe working conditions; provide PPE; report incidents | DOLE compliance; site audit | CAP; training requirement |
| Anti-Corruption | Zero tolerance for bribery, extortion, facilitation payments | Self-certification + audit | Immediate termination |
| Conflict Minerals | Disclose use of tin, tantalum, tungsten, gold (3TG) from conflict-affected regions | CMRT (Conflict Minerals Reporting Template) | Termination if undisclosed |
| Data Privacy | Comply with RA 10173 (Data Privacy Act) | Self-certification | CAP |
| Timber Legality | All timber products from legal sources; FSC/PEFC preferred | Chain of custody documentation | Termination if illegal timber |
| Chemical Safety | Comply with RA 6969; provide SDS for all chemical products | SDS review; lab testing | Product suspension until compliant |
| Animal Welfare | No animal testing for products where alternatives exist | Self-certification | CAP |
